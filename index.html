Game Minefun Io Details

MineFun.io is a fun online game that takes you into a colorful blocky world full of adventures! Start in parkour mode, where you jump, run, and dodge tricky obstacles.

Choose from lots of cool maps, like island fun lands or the classic Obby world. Collect coins to unlock awesome skins and make your character your own.

Want to play with friends? Create a private room or share your room code to team up. Are you ready to join the fun?

How to play Minefun Io

Move: WASD or the arrow keys

Jump: the space bar

Crouch: C
